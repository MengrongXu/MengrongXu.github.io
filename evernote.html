Evernote IMC Campaign

Content Development / Strategy Thinking

Evernote is one of the most popular cloud note-taking applications. It is the early-mover of this category, but nowadays, more similar applications appear and it is hard to distinguish them from functionalities. How can we distinguish Evernote from its competiors? We developed an integrated marketing campaign for Evernote.

The theme is "Evernote, forever note." We aim to reposition it as a useful efficiency tool for work rather than other uses. We connect the Chinese meaning "印象" with memory. The theme of this campaign is "万事有印象" （Evernote, forever note.)
