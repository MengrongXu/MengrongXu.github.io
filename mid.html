Design: Mid-Autumn E-card

Multimedia / Content Development

This is an E-card designed for Mid-Autumn Festival. It is a traditional Chinese festival for family reunions, having a long history and an abundance of stories. I draw inspiration from the Chinese poem "海上生明月，天涯共此時“. Then I integrated traditional Mid-Autumn elements like the rabbits, osmanthus

trees and lanterns to create the image of Mid-Autumn Day. The full moon is the visual center of the layout. Meanwhile, to express the message of reunion, there is a family of rabbits and the trees appear by couple.

Get a reunion on Mid-Autumn.
