An Analysis of Raw Data: Perspective of Umbrella Model

Analytics / Strategy Thinking

Virtual Reality has become a heated technology and been applied to various industries. In this report, we focus on a popular VR game: Raw Data. Raw Data is one of the most popular game designed by Survios Studio. We adopted Umbrella Model as a framework for the analysis. This is a comprehensive perspective

which suggests that new media should always be discussed under cirtain contexts. It includes four levels: hardware & software, organizational infrastructure, system level and individual level. This report is by teamwork, I was mainly in charge of the introduction and the system level analysis.
