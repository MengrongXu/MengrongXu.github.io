A UX Analysis: Booking vs Agoda

Analytics

Mobile navigation applications have become important tools for our life. There are various productis in this category. AMap(高德地圖) & Baidu Map(百度地圖) are leaders of mobile navigation market in mainland China. The competition is quite fierce, therefore, it is essential to conduct users studies. Knowing what users are seeking for will enhance the insights.

To obtain the data, I launched a questionnaire on sojump.com(問卷星) and 60 valid respondes were collected. The questionnaire and analysis was leveraged on the Multi-Attribute Attitudes Models, which was a useful approach for measuring user attitudes. The most important factor for users was navigation. Gender & uses senarios also influenced the choices.
